Dragon Slayers

Players will have their names, weapons, and classes randomly as they press each button. When players get their names and weapons classes randomly, they could kill the dragon. When players press “Let’s kill the dragon” button, players’ name and weapon class follow players, and every time players kill a dragon, players get different results.I felt a lot like our lives through this project. Throughout our lives, we can encounter unwanted situations or results but we can humbly accept those consequences and things and move forward. I wanted to convey these words through my project.
