Get My Identity

This digital artwork is a randomizer that helps you to find your identity if you were Korean, called Get my Identity. I throw a question to the audience, have you ever wondered about having your other name or identity? When you click the “Get my Identity,” the imaginary being will tell your randomized Korean name and its meaning. The very first page with abstract symbolized texts contains a description of Korean national culture. I got inspired by the movie Matrix's first scene when Trinity talks through the computer to Neo, saying, "Hi, Neo.” People sometimes desire to be someone else. For instance, people are living with different identities online. Therefore, I brought this contemporary concept to my work and incorporated it. In this artwork, I am an assistant to users to guide them to explore digital media experience.
